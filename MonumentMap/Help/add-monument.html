Adding a new monument

Adding a new monument basically consists from the following three steps

  • Clicking on the add new monument icon
  • Filling out data regarding the new monument
  • Saving the monument

Click on the add new monument icon

Adding a new monument starts by clicking on the following icon: . The icon is located in the upper left corner of the window. If you are feeling a little lazy, you could always use the "CTRL + N" shortcut to perform the same thing.
